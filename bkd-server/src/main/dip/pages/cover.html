Welcome to DIP - The Database of Interacting Proteins

The Database of Interacting Proteins archives and evaluates experimentally determined interactions between proteins. The interactions in DIP are culled from peer-reviewed literature and are manually entered into the database by expert curators.

DIP is a member of The International Molecular-interaction Exchange Consortium (IMEx), a group of the major public providers of interaction data that includes IntAct, MINT and others. The IMEx databases work together to prevent duplication of curation efforts and to maintain consistency of the resulting interaction data they share and maintain.
