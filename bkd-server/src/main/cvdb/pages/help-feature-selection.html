Sequence Variant Help: Feature Selection

This search box allows you to highlight a specific position of interest.

  • Using the drop down menu, select Protein or Genome
  • In the position of interest box, enter either the amino acid number or genomic coordinate.
  • Clicking on set will highlight that position in the graph below. Only one position of interest can be highlighted at a time.
  • The position of interest will also be highlighted in the panel on the right, including the genome viewer, homology, membrane topology, and SwissModel tabs. This allows you to see where the position of interest falls on the protein structure.
  • You can removing the highlighting by clicking on Clear or by entering a new position of interest and clicking Set.
