Welcome to Channel Variants of Unknown Significance (CVUS) Database

CVUS Database is designed to simplify access to information about known missense variants of selected Human voltage gated channels, channels transporters and related proteins, and present it in the context of genomic and proteomic data.

The database currently covers:

Voltage Gated Potassium Channels:
    KCNJ1, KCNJ2, KCNJ3, KCNJ4, ...

Voltage Gated Sodium Channels:
    SCN1A, ...

Accessory Proteins
    CALM1, CALM2, CALM3, ...
