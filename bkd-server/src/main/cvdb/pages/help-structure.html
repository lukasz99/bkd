Help: Structure Viewer

3D structures (experimenal or predicted) are visualised in NGL Viewer.

Basic Structure Manipulation

  • Left Mouse Button (Hold): Rotate the structure around center.
  • Right Mouse Button (Drag): Pan the structure or translates the structure across the screen.
  • Scroll Wheel: Zoom in and out on the structure
  • Single Left Mouse Click: pick an atom
  • Double Left Mouse Click: Center the view on a clicked atom or residue.

Selection and Measurement

  • Ctrl + Left Mouse Button (Click): Select atoms, residues, or other objects. To measure the distance between atoms select two positions (as in 1) and right click
  • Shift + Right Mouse Button (Click): Deselect objects.
