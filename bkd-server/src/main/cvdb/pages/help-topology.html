Help: Membrane Topology

The membrane topology of protein is visualized via diagrams generated with the web-based tool Protter.

  • The plasma membrane is shown in orange
  • Transmembrane regions (helices) are indicated in blue Roman numerals
  • Residues selected within the lollipop panel will appear in orange color on the membrane topology panel
